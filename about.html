About Booth Drop

Creating memorable moments for over 10 years

Our Story

Booth Drop was founded with a simple mission: to create unforgettable memories at events. What started as a small venture in Luton has grown into a trusted name for professional photo booth hire across the UK.

With over 10 years of experience and more than 500 events completed, we've learned what it takes to make your special day even more special. From intimate gatherings to large corporate events, we bring the same level of professionalism and attention to detail to every booking.

Our team is passionate about photography and event technology, and we're dedicated to providing the best possible experience for both you and your guests.
