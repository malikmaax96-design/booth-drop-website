Booking FAQs

What's included in the package price?

All packages include setup/breakdown, attendant for the duration, instant prints, and digital copies. Premium add-ons are additional.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfer, card payment via PayPal, or cheque. A £99 deposit secures your booking, with balance due 1 week before your event.

Can I reschedule my booking?

Yes! We allow rescheduling up to 7 days before your event at no charge. After that, we may charge a rescheduling fee.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 14+ days before your event get a full refund. Less than 14 days: deposit is non-refundable but transferable to another date.

Do you require a contract?

Yes, we'll send a simple contract after booking confirmation outlining all terms and details.

Can I book same-day or rush bookings?

Contact us directly at +44 (0) 1582 365252 for rush bookings! We may be able to accommodate.
