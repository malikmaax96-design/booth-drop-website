Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the photo booth cost?

Our pricing varies depending on the type of event, duration, and location. Wedding packages start from £399, corporate events from £349, and parties from £299. Contact us for a custom quote!

What's included in the package?

All packages include professional setup, 4x6 instant prints, digital copies of all photos, backdrop design, and our friendly operator for the duration of your event.

How far do you travel?

We serve Luton and up to 60 miles radius, covering areas including Milton Keynes, Watford, Hemel Hempstead, St Albans, Bedford, Stevenage, and London.

Can you customize the backdrop?

Absolutely! We offer custom backdrops with your logos, colors, or themes. This is perfect for branded corporate events or personalized celebrations.

How do I book?

Simply visit our booking page, fill in your event details, and we'll confirm availability. You can also call us at +44 (0) 1582 365252.

What if I need to reschedule?

We offer flexible rescheduling up to 7 days before your event with no penalty. Please visit our contact page to arrange changes.
