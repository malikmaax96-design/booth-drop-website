Frequently Asked Questions

How much does photo booth hire cost?

Our packages start from just £299 for 3 hours. Wedding packages from £349, corporate events from £449. All prices include unlimited prints, attendant, props, digital album & setup. Contact us for a custom quote!

What's included in every package?

Every single hire includes: totally unlimited prints, a trained friendly attendant, a fun props bucket, a digital album of all photos, custom branded print templates, and full delivery & setup. No hidden extras.

How far do you travel?

We're based in Luton and cover up to 60 miles — including Milton Keynes, Watford, Hemel Hempstead, St Albans, Bedford, Stevenage, Birmingham and London. Get in touch if you're outside this area.

Can you brand the booth for corporate events?

Absolutely! Our corporate package includes custom booth branding (exterior wrap), branded touchscreen interface, logo on every print-out, and even data capture & social media sharing features.

How do I book?

Simply visit our booking page, fill in your event details, and we'll confirm availability within 24 hours. Or call us directly at +44 (0) 1582 365252.
